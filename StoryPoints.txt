User stories and story points

- As a user you can log in 1pts
- As a user I can send/recieve messages 5pt


- As a player you can register 1pts
- As a player I can to manage my collection 5pts
	- As a player I can to view my collection 2pts
	- As a player I can add/remove cards to my collection 3pts
- As a player I can view a store 1pts
- As a player I can purchase cards from a store 2pts
- As a player I can make a trade offer 1pt
- As a player I can accept/reject a trade offer 2pt
- As a player I can view other players inventory 2pts
- As a player I can search for a specific card across stores/players 5pts


- As a store owner I can manage my inventory 5pts
	- As a store owner I can add/remove cards to my inventory 3pts
	- As a store owner I can view my inventory 2pts
	- As a store owner I can modify my inventory 1pt
- As a store owner I want to feature a card on my storefront 1pt
